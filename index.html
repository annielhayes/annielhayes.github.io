Annie Hayes

Hello! My name is Annie Hayes. I am a sixteen year old junior in high school at Bob Jones Academy.
My weekdays consist of drinking Starbucks Iced Chai Lattes, doing yoga on stressful afternoons, and
running cross country at BJA.

On Fridays and Saturdays, I usually am hanging out with my best friend Maria, editing pictures to post
on my VSCO, or watching Netflix when I should be studying.

Sundays, I go to my home church, Faith Baptist in Taylors! Apples would have to be my favorite food.
I am always reading and planning what adventure I should go on next, and at the end of September I’m
going up to Washington D.C.

My favorite season would have to be Autumn: the colors, big pumpkins, knit sweaters, corn mazes…
what's not to love!? I am currently gluten-free because I loved bread way too much. I hate coffee,
and I don’t enjoy chocolate. My favorite passage of Scripture is Psalm 126!

Psalm 126
A song for pilgrims ascending to Jerusalem.

1. When the Lord brought back his exiles
to Jerusalem, it was like a dream!

2. We were filled with laughter,
and we sang for joy.

And the other nations said,
“What amazing things
the Lord has done for them.”

3. Yes, the Lord has done
amazing things for us!
What joy!

4. Restore our fortunes, Lord,
as streams renew the desert.

5. Those who plant in tears
will harvest with shouts of joy.

6. They weep as they go to plant their seed,
but they sing as they return with the harvest.
